Spindown Presets
Spindown timeout values: The value sets idle time before the drive spins down. Common values: 0 = disabled, 60 = 5 minutes, 120 = 10 minutes, 240 = 20 minutes, 241 = 30 minutes, 242 = 1 hour, 244 = 2 hours. Values 1–240 are multiples of 5 seconds. Values 241–251 are multiples of 30 minutes. Settings persist across reboots via /etc/dplaneos/power-management.json.
